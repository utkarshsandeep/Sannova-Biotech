About Us

“ We would like to introduce Sannova Biotech Pvt. Limited, a reliable supplier and manufacturing service provider of wide range of pharmaceutical products under state of art manufacturing criteria. We also offer pharma franchise in India with complete range of promotional and marketing support.”

We are providing third party manufacturing services for Pharmaceutical Tablets, Capsules, Liquid Orals, Dry Syrups, Suspensions, Injectables, etc.
We welcome inquiries for third party manufacturing basis under their given brand names. We consider big as well as small batch sizes as per client’s requirement at lowest possible prices.
Sannova has attained the commendable position in this domain due to our ability to meet the bulk order within the assured time constraints. The customer-centric approach and transparent business dealings, which are implemented in our organization since its inception, have helped us in gaining the trust of our valuable customers in the most efficient manner.Our capabilities also lie in assisting the clients to formulate guidelines that allow them to promote their products in the market, and further increasing their presence.
